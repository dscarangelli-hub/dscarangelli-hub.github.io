About WSUA

WSUA was founded to build a living bridge of solidarity between the Western United States and Ukraine. Our emblem reflects our identity: Washington’s evergreen roots intertwined with Ukraine’s viburnum — two cultures connected in purpose.

Our Values

  • Transparency — every donation is traceable
  • Solidarity — unity across continents
  • Action — real support, real impact

Our Story

WSUA began as a grassroots effort to support Ukrainian communities during crisis. Today, we continue that mission with integrity, clarity, and a commitment to long-term partnership.
