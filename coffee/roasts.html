WSUA Coffee Roasts

Crafted with purpose. Inspired by Ukraine. Roasted for solidarity.

Our Signature Roasts

Each WSUA roast is inspired by a Ukrainian city, story, or symbol of resilience. These coffees are crafted to honor the people and places that continue to stand strong.

Ghostpresso of Kyiv

Espresso Roast

A bold, fearless espresso inspired by the legendary defender of Kyiv’s skies. Dark, intense, and unforgettable — crafted for those who rise to the moment.

Zaporizhzhia Sich

Dark Roast

A deep, powerful dark blend honoring the historic Zaporizhzhian Sich — the fortified heart of Cossack independence. Smoky, rich, and resilient, this roast pays tribute to centuries of Ukrainian courage.

Mykolaiv Morning

Medium Roast

Bright, balanced, and steady — like the spirit of Mykolaiv, a city that refused to fall. Notes of warmth and clarity for a hopeful start to any day.

Colombian Solidarity

100% Colombian

A smooth, vibrant Colombian roast created in solidarity with global partners who stand with Ukraine. Bright acidity, caramel sweetness, and a clean finish.
