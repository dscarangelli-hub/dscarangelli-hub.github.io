WSUA mobilizes communities across the Western United States to support Ukraine through humanitarian aid, legal research, and energy independence for rural communities. We work to protect the rights of displaced Ukrainians, strengthen accountability efforts, and build the foundations for long‑term recovery and restitution.

Pursuing 501(c)(3) status · Transparent reporting · EIN 41-359-3491 · UBI 606-029-374

What We Do

Humanitarian Aid

Emergency relief, medical supplies, and direct support for displaced families.

Reconstruction

Legal and logistical research supporting the rebuilding of homes and infrastructure.

Energy Maidan Project

Helping rural communities achieve energy independence with generators, solar, and more.

Diaspora Support

Legal guidance and empowerment for Ukrainians across the Western U.S.
