Ways You Can Support Ukraine

Supporting Ukraine can take many forms: financial donations, advocacy, volunteering, and staying informed. WSUA helps connect communities in the Western United States with vetted, effective ways to make a difference.

Donate

Financial support helps deliver humanitarian aid, fund legal research, and sustain programs like the Energy Maidan Project. Every contribution counts. Give online, via bank transfer, or by scanning our QR code.

Volunteer

Offer your time and skills—from logistics and research to outreach and creative work. Apply to volunteer with WSUA and we’ll match you with opportunities.

Advocate & Stay Informed

Share reliable information, contact elected representatives, and support policies that advance humanitarian relief and justice for Ukraine. Subscribe to our newsletter for updates and ways to engage.

Partner & Amplify

If you represent an organization, consider partnering with WSUA or amplifying our work to your network. We work with trusted Ukrainian and international partners to maximize impact.
