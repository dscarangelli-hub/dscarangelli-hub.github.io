Why Legal Work Matters

WSUA’s legal work focuses on reparations, restitution, property rights, and transitional justice for Ukraine. Our goal is to make complex legal frameworks understandable and actionable for affected communities, practitioners, and advocates.

Key Themes

  • Reparations: Financial and non‑financial redress for harms suffered.
  • Restitution: Restoring property, rights, and status where possible.
  • Transitional Justice: Truth, accountability, and institutional reform.
  • Property Rights: Protecting homes, land, and livelihoods during and after war.
