Choosing Where to Donate to Ukraine

Many organizations are doing vital work for Ukraine. When you give, it’s important to choose groups that are transparent, accountable, and aligned with your values. WSUA is one option—we focus on humanitarian aid, legal research, and the Energy Maidan Project for rural energy independence.

Why Donate Through WSUA?

  • Humanitarian aid — emergency relief, medical supplies, and support for displaced families.
  • Legal research — reparations, restitution, and claims guidance for Ukrainians.
  • Energy Maidan Project — generators, solar panels, and energy independence for rural communities.
  • Transparency — we publish our approach, documents, and commitment to accountability. See our Transparency page.

WSUA is pursuing 501(c)(3) status. Donations are not yet tax‑deductible. EIN 41-359-3491 · Washington UBI 606-029-374.

Other Vetted Options

We encourage you to research other reputable organizations working in and for Ukraine—UN agencies, established NGOs, and community-led initiatives. Give to groups that clearly state their mission, show how funds are used, and align with your goals.
