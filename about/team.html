Ukrainian Counterpart Organization

WSUA works exclusively with its Ukrainian counterpart organization for all on-the-ground humanitarian coordination, verification, and community engagement inside Ukraine. This organization is fully independent and is not part of WSUA’s governance structure, but serves as our trusted operational partner within Ukraine.

Solidarity With Ukraine Foundation

Exclusive Ukrainian Counterpart Organization

The Solidarity With Ukraine Foundation is WSUA’s exclusive Ukrainian counterpart for on-the-ground humanitarian work. The foundation supports displaced families, frontline communities, and local initiatives, providing local insight and operational capacity across Ukraine.
